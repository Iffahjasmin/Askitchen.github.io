ABOUT US

Drawing inspiration from various cafes around the world after a long fulfilling life of travel, we at A`S Kitchen aim to provide an excitement like no other paired with a rewarding experience that is indeed unique with every visit to our humble abode.

We strive to provide our beloved customers with the best quality service and delicious eats ranging from delectable small bites to exquisite main courses complemented with a flavorful coffee. It is our mission to make your time here a pleasant memory with a pleasant experience like no other.

The story behind our cafe narrates a tale of love for food and a passion for travel which eventually sparked a dream that led to the birth of A`S Kitchen After careful study of an illustrious collection eateries scattered around the globe, A`S Kitchen started its baby steps by merging different ideas in hopes of forming an authentic experience which caters to all walks of life. Whether you’re coming in for a business meeting, a casual drink or a lovely dinner with friends and family, we’d like to share our love for good meals and coffee surrounded by a vibrant energy complemented by a comfortable ambience. Feel free to drop by.
